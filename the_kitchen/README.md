# The Kitchen

- Year created: 2010
- Author: [Brian Ustas](http://brianustas.com)

Maya, Mental Ray, ZBrush, PhotoShop, UV Layout

3D rendering of my family's kitchen in South Africa.

All assets were modeled and textured by myself. It took me 2 years...
